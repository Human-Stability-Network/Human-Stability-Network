Escalation Protocol

Tier 1: AI Self-Service (90% of requests)

· Resolved within 2 minutes
· No human involvement
· Participant satisfaction target: 4.5/5

Tier 2: AI + Human Review (8% of requests)

· AI drafts solution
· Human reviews (15 minutes SLA)
· Human approves, edits, or rejects
· Participant satisfaction target: 4.8/5

Tier 3: Human Specialist (2% of requests)

· Immediate transfer to queue
· 2-hour maximum wait
· 4-hour resolution target
· Participant satisfaction target: 4.9/5
