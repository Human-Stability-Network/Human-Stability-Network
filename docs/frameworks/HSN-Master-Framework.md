## Core Principles

1. **Dignity as Default** - Programs assume capability, not deficit
2. **No Wrong Door** - Any entry point connects to all services
3. **AI-Funded Sustainability** - Value engine covers operating costs
4. **Privacy Preserving** - Data belongs to participants
5. **Intergenerational Impact** - Break cycles, don't manage them

## Program Tiers

| Tier | Target | Duration | AI Support |
|------|--------|----------|------------|
| Tier 1: Crisis | Immediate needs | 0-3 months | High |
| Tier 2: Stability | Core programs | 3-12 months | Medium |
| Tier 3: Independence | Financial/employment | 12-24 months | Low |
| Tier 4: Thriving | Alumni support | Ongoing | Optional |

## Participant Journey
