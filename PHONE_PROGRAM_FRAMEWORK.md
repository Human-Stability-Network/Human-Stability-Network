# Unlimited Phone Program Framework

## Mission

Provide every participant with a permanent phone number and unlimited connectivity as a lifeline to stability.

## Program Components

### 1. Device Provisioning
- Smartphone (Android or iOS, refurbished)
- Charger and case
- Screen protector
- Quick start guide

### 2. Unlimited Service
- Unlimited talk (domestic)
- Unlimited text (SMS/MMS)
- Unlimited data (5G/LTE where available)
- Hotspot capability (20GB/month)

### 3. Permanent Number
- Number remains active for life
- Voicemail to email transcription
- Call forwarding (any device)

### 4. Support Services
- 24/7 tech support (AI + human)
- Device replacement (lost/damaged)
- Accessibility accommodations

## Eligibility

- All enrolled participants
- Children 13+ receive own device
- Children under 13 on family plan

## Number Portability

Participants keep their HSN number even after leaving the program (with reduced service tier).

## AI Support Integration

- **Spam blocking** - AI filters robocalls
- **Appointment reminders** - Syncs with calendar
- **Language translation** - Real-time call translation
- **Emergency alerts** - Weather, safety notifications

## Service Tiers

| Tier | Data Speed | Hotspot | Video Calls | Use Case |
|------|------------|---------|-------------|----------|
| Standard | 5 Mbps | 5GB/month | Yes | Daily use |
| Enhanced | 25 Mbps | 20GB/month | Yes | Remote work |
| Unlimited | Full speed | 50GB/month | Yes | Power user |

## Success Metrics

- Connectivity rate: >99% uptime
- Number retention: >95% year over year
- Support resolution: <4 hours average

## Carrier Partnerships

- Primary: T-Mobile (Project 10Million)
- Secondary: AT&T (affordable connectivity)
- Backup: Regional carriers
