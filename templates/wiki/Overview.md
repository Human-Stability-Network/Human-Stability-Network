## What is HSN?

The Human Stability Network is a nonprofit ecosystem that provides essentials (housing, food, connectivity, healthcare, education) to families and children, funded by an AI Value Engine that creates dignified digital work.

## The Two-Sided Coin

AI safety requires two things:

1. **AI Guardrails** – Safety inside the technology
2. **Human Stability** – Safety in real life

HSN builds the second side of the coin.

## Core Principles

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Dignity First | Programs assume capability, not deficit |
| No Wrong Door | Any entry point connects to all services |
| AI-Funded | Value engine covers operating costs |
| Privacy Preserving | Data belongs to participants |
| Intergenerational | Break cycles, don't manage them |
