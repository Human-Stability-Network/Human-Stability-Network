# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is the Human Stability Network?
HSN is a nonprofit that provides housing, food, phones, computers, healthcare, and education support – all funded by an AI Value Engine that creates dignified digital work.

### Who is eligible?
Anyone experiencing instability. We prioritize families with children but serve all ages.

### Is there a cost?
No. All HSN programs are free to participants.

### How is HSN funded?
The AI Value Engine generates revenue through safe digital tasks (data labeling, transcription, etc.). 60% of revenue funds programs.

## Programs

### What housing support is available?
Emergency shelter, short-term rental subsidies, long-term assistance, and down payment help.

### Do I get to keep the phone?
Yes. Your HSN phone and number are yours permanently.

### What kind of computer do I get?
A laptop (or desktop) suitable for work and education, plus internet access.

### Is healthcare really included?
Yes – medical, dental, and mental health access pathways with navigation support.

## Youth & Trust Fund

### How does the Trust Fund work?
Every enrolled child gets a locked savings account. Contributions come from AI Value Engine earnings, educational milestones, and matching programs. Funds unlock at age 18-21 for education, housing, or career launch.

### Can parents withdraw from the Trust Fund?
No. The account is protected until the youth reaches the access age.

### What happens if a child leaves HSN?
The Trust Fund remains. The child keeps their account and any accumulated funds.

## AI Value Engine

### What kind of work do participants do?
Safe digital tasks like image labeling, audio transcription, and AI training data review. No harmful content, no PII exposure.

### How much can I earn?
Average $12-15/hour. Maximum 40 hours/week.

### Do I need experience?
No. The AI copilot guides you through every task.

## Privacy

### Is my data safe?
Yes. All data encrypted, access logged, never sold.

### Who can see my information?
Only your assigned navigator and necessary program staff. You control sharing with partners.

### What about my children's data?
Same protections apply. Stricter rules for minors.

## Getting Help

### How do I apply?
Visit hsn.org/apply, call 1-888-HSN-CARE, or visit a community partner.

### What if I don't have internet?
Call us. We'll help you apply by phone.

### Is support available 24/7?
Yes. AI support is always available. Human support during business hours, crisis line 24/7.

## Volunteering & Donations

### Can I volunteer?
Yes. Visit hsn.org/volunteer for opportunities.

### How do I donate?
Donations accepted at hsn.org/donate. All donations go directly to participant services.

### Are donations tax-deductible?
Yes. HSN is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit.
