# Medical, Dental & Mental Health Access Framework

## Mission

Remove every barrier to healthcare access so participants can heal and build stability.

## Program Components

### 1. Medical Care
- **Primary care navigation** - Appointments, transportation
- **Specialist referrals** - Coordinated scheduling
- **Medication access** - Copay assistance, mail-order pharmacy
- **Emergency care** - ER navigation, bill advocacy

### 2. Dental Care
- **Preventive** - Cleanings (2x/year), exams, X-rays
- **Restorative** - Fillings, extractions, crowns
- **Emergency** - Pain management, abscess treatment
- **Orthodontics** - Children only, medical necessity

### 3. Mental Health Care
- **Counseling** - Weekly therapy (individual/family)
- **Psychiatry** - Medication management
- **Crisis support** - 24/7 hotline, mobile response
- **Support groups** - Peer-led, condition-specific
- **Trauma-informed care** - Specialized providers

## AI Health Navigator Capabilities

- **Symptom checking** - Triage (not diagnosis)
- **Provider matching** - Insurance, language, specialty
- **Appointment scheduling** - Calendar integration
- **Medication reminders** - Push notifications
- **Record requests** - Digital medical records
- **Bill review** - Identifies errors, negotiates

## Mental Health Tiers

| Tier | Need Level | Intervention | Frequency |
|------|------------|--------------|-----------|
| 1 | Mild | Self-guided app, support group | Daily app |
| 2 | Moderate | Weekly therapy, coaching | 1x/week |
| 3 | Severe | Therapy + psychiatry | 2x/week + monthly |
| 4 | Crisis | Mobile crisis, inpatient | As needed |

## Child-Specific Mental Health

- **School-based services** - On-campus counseling
- **Play therapy** - Age-appropriate modalities
- **Family therapy** - Systemic support
- **Trauma-focused CBT** - Evidence-based protocol

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Primary care within 30 days | >95% |
| Dental visit within 90 days | >80% |
| Therapy attendance | >85% |
| Medication adherence | >90% |
| Crisis episode reduction | 50% year over year |

## Privacy Protections

- HIPAA-compliant systems
- Separate consent for mental health records
- No health data shared with other HSN programs without explicit consent
- Minor consent protocols (age-appropriate)
