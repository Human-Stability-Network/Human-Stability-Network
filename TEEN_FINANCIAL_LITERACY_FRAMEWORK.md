# Teen Financial Literacy Track Framework

## Mission

Prepare every teen for financial independence through practical education, hands-on experience, and protected asset building.

## Program Components

### 1. Core Curriculum (9 modules)

| Module | Topics | Duration |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Money Foundations | Earning, saving, spending | 2 weeks |
| Banking Basics | Accounts, fees, interest | 2 weeks |
| Budgeting | Tracking, planning, tools | 2 weeks |
| Credit & Debt | Scores, cards, loans | 2 weeks |
| Investing | Stocks, bonds, compound interest | 2 weeks |
| Taxes | Filing, withholding, refunds | 1 week |
| Insurance | Health, auto, renters | 1 week |
| Consumer Skills | Comparison shopping, scams | 1 week |
| Career & Income | Negotiation, benefits | 2 weeks |

### 2. Hands-On Experience
- **Teen debit account** - Parent/guardian co-signed
- **Budgeting app** - Real spending tracking
- **Savings challenges** - Matching program (2:1 up to $500)
- **Simulation games** - Stock market, life events

### 3. Trust Fund Integration
- Financial literacy completion unlocks Trust Fund access
- Milestone bonuses for demonstrated skills
- Portfolio review with financial coach at 18

### 4. Career Connections
- Part-time job placement (15-20 hours/week)
- Internship pathways
- Entrepreneurship micro-grants ($500)
- Tax preparation assistance

## Age-Appropriate Delivery

| Age | Content Depth | Practical Component |
|-----|---------------|---------------------|
| 13-14 | Foundations | Allowance budgeting |
| 14-15 | Intermediate | Part-time job earnings |
| 15-16 | Advanced | Savings goals |
| 16-17 | Complex | Credit simulation |
| 17-18 | Real-world | Tax filing practice |

## AI Support Features

- **Personalized coaching** - Chatbot for daily questions
- **Spending insights** - Categorizes and analyzes
- **Goal tracking** - Visual progress toward targets
- **Scenario planning** - "What if" financial models
- **Fraud alerts** - Identifies potential scams

## Certifications

- **HSN Financial Fundamentals** (completion of 9 modules)
- **Certified Youth Financial Coach** (peer mentor track)
- **Tax Preparation Assistant** (volunteer opportunity)

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Curriculum completion | >85% | Module assessments |
| Savings rate | >20% of earnings | Account data |
| Budget adherence | Within 10% | App tracking |
| Credit knowledge | 80% correct | Pre/post test |
| Trust Fund growth | $5k by 18 | Account balance |

## Parent/Guardian Role

- Co-sign for teen account
- Attend quarterly reviews
- Model financial behaviors
- No withdrawal access to teen Trust Fund
